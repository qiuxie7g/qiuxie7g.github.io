A Chinese Factory Girl Turned A Stripper Chasing An Acting Dream

The Documentary film focuses on the young generation's dream-chasing journey in Modern China. A former factory girl, 22-year-old Jolin is the only child of her family, who has found work as a stripper in Dongguan. She undergoes risky plastic surgery to look more 'sexy' and tries to find her estranged father for reasons that go beyond healing her fractured family. She wants to escape from the manufacturing and sex industries, dreams of becoming an actress in Shanghai. Jolin's story depicts a young girl's "Chinese Dream" and the sadness of China's loneliest generation.
